The world's longest glass-bottom bridge opens in Vietnam.

The Cau Rong bridge in Da Nang, Vietnam, opened in June 2022. The bridge is 632 meters long and has a glass bottom that allows visitors to see the water 150 meters below.

16 Dec

The world's longest footbridge opened in the Czech Republic..

The Sky Bridge 721 in the Czech Republic opened in July 2022. The bridge is 721 meters long and has a height of 1,125 feet. It is the longest suspension footbridge in the world.

The construction of the world's first floating bridge in Singapore.

The Singapore Floating Bridge is under construction and is expected to be completed in 2024. The bridge will be 2.2 kilometers long and will connect the islands of Sentosa and Pulau Brani.
